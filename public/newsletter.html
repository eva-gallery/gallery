Dear Friends,

We hope this message finds you well, and that you've had some time to dive in and experience the EVA Gallery platform following its official launch earlier this year. It was a joy to celebrate our community during the Awarding Ceremony - thank you again to everyone who participated and contributed to making the launch of the European Visual Arts Gallery a success! We would like to showcase selected artworks of our winners within this newsletter.

Georges Digalakis (GR) - 1st Prize winner

Since the launch, our technical team has been working on fixing some bugs and improving system performance for a better user experience, particularly regarding the 3D Gallery Designer and the Artwork Uploading functions. We have been hard at work preparing proposals for further development through open calls such as the Creative Europe and EIT Culture & Creativity, because we want to keep EVA Gallery growing into a lasting digital hub for the European visual art scene.

Andreas Varro (SWE) - 2nd Prize winner

As a friendly reminder, here's what the EVA Gallery enables you to do now:

  • Showcase your artworks in a customizable 3D virtual exhibition space
  • Protect your art and digital IP (intellectual property) using AI-powered tools designed to prevent unauthorized use
  • Create and mint NFTs directly through the platform
  • Browse the profiles of fellow artists and galleries from across all Europe

Natalia Philippou (CY) - 3rd Prize winner

The platform is still in its early phase, and your continued use and feedback are incredibly valuable as we shape what EVA Gallery will become. If you experience any technical issues or have suggestions for future features, feel free to reach out to us anytime at support@evagallery.eu.

We'll be sharing more updates soon about upcoming improvements, funding outcomes, and new opportunities to get involved. Thank you once again for being part of this exciting research project, and supporting the digital transformation of European visual arts.


Our team is here to help you with any inquiries.
